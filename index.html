About Dr. Neelam Mishra

Rehabilitation Psychologist & Clinical Psychologist

Your Trusted Psychology Partner

Dr. Neelam Mishra is a Rehabilitation Psychologist and Clinical Psychologist, RCI registered by the Government of India. She is one of the very few doctors who are registered in Varanasi, bringing years of dedicated experience in providing compassionate mental health care.

Located in Sigra-Mahmoorganj Road, a friendly and prime location in Varanasi, our clinic offers a very positive vibe with facilities like toilets for clients and patients. With a commitment to excellence and a client-centered approach, Dr. Mishra ensures that each individual receives personalized attention and evidence-based therapeutic interventions tailored to their unique needs. Our practice provides all types of therapies, counselling, and services for Stress, Anxiety, Depression, OCD, Relationship Issues, Behavioral Issues, Migraine, Adjustment Disorder, Mental Disorders, and many more.
